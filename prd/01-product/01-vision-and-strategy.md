# Vision & Strategy

> "Consistencia es la unica metrica que importa."

## Mission

Ordo Creator OS is the **operating system for content creators**. It replaces the chaos of managing a one-person media company with an opinionated, structured workflow that drives consistency and growth.

## The Problem

Content creators are running **one-person media companies** without the tools of a media company. They juggle scripting, filming, editing, community management, analytics, sponsorships, and publishing across dozens of platforms -- all while trying to stay creative.

**Current solutions fail them:**

| Tool | Why It Fails |
|------|-------------|
| Todoist / TickTick | Too generic. Doesn't understand the content lifecycle |
| Notion | Blank canvas creates analysis paralysis. Too slow for daily use |
| Apple / Google Notes | Ideas captured but die without actionable structure |
| Spreadsheets | Manual tracking, no automation, no creative workflow |
| Multiple SaaS tools | Context switching kills flow and consistency |

## The Solution

Ordo is **opinionated software**. It doesn't ask "what do you want to do?" -- it provides a **proven workflow** and gets out of the way.

### Four Pillars

#### 1. Frictionless Capture
Ideas die in seconds. Capture must be instant.
- WhatsApp/Telegram bot capture
- Global keyboard shortcuts (Cmd+K)
- Voice capture with AI transcription
- Mobile quick-capture widget
- **Target: Idea captured in < 5 seconds**

#### 2. The Content Pipeline
Every piece of content follows a predictable lifecycle.
- Kanban-based workflow: Idea > Scripting > Filming > Editing > Review > Scheduled > Published
- Type-aware cards (Video, Tweet, Newsletter, Podcast)
- Asset management per piece
- Smart checklists generated by AI
- Series management for episodic content

#### 3. Creators Studio AI
Context-aware AI that augments (never replaces) human creativity.
- Block-based editor with AI sidebar
- Brainstormer: 1 topic > 10 angles
- Script Doctor: Retention analysis and hook optimization
- Title/Thumbnail Lab: CTR-optimized variations
- Repurposing Engine: 1 video > 5 platform-specific pieces

#### 4. Data-Driven Growth
Consistency is gamified. Growth is measurable.
- Consistency Score with streaks and multipliers
- GitHub-style creation heatmap
- Unified cross-platform analytics
- Audience persona-driven content strategy
- Income tracking and sponsorship CRM

## Target Audience

### Primary: Solo Content Creators (1-50K followers)
- YouTubers, TikTokers, newsletter writers, podcasters
- Ages 22-38
- Tech-savvy, mobile-first
- Publishing 2-5 pieces/week across 2-3 platforms
- Revenue: $0-10K/month from content

### Secondary: Small Creator Teams (2-5 people)
- Creator + editor + social media manager
- Need collaboration and approval workflows
- Revenue: $10K-100K/month

### Tertiary: Creator Agencies
- Managing 5-20 creators
- Need workspace isolation and client management
- Revenue: $100K+/month

## Product Principles

1. **Consistency Over Perfection**: Ship regularly > ship perfectly
2. **Opinionated by Default**: Best practices built-in, customizable when needed
3. **Speed is a Feature**: Every interaction under 100ms perceived latency
4. **Creator-First Design**: Built for the creative mind, not the corporate one
5. **AI Augments, Humans Decide**: AI provides options, creator makes choices
6. **Data Without Overwhelm**: Surface insights, hide complexity
7. **Platform Parity**: Same core experience on Web, Mobile, Desktop
